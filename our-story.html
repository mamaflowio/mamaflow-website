Our Story

Built by someone
who needed it.

MamaFlow didn't start as a business idea. It started as a frustration. A mum who had figured out how to take care of herself — and then had a baby and found that nothing out there was built for where she was.

The founder

Tendai

I'm a corporate professional. I coached myself from 85kg down to 73kg before pregnancy — and since my c-section I've gone from 100kg to 84kg. No fads. Just figuring out that healthy can actually taste good and consistency beats intensity every single time.

But postpartum, with a newborn and a full life suddenly restructured around someone else's needs, I had no idea how to show up for myself in a way that made sense for the day I was actually having. Time poor, sleep deprived, recovering from major surgery — and everything I found either ignored the fact I'd had major abdominal surgery, or was built for someone with two hours and a home gym.

None of it was built for me.

"I couldn't find it.
So I'm building it."

MamaFlow is what I wish had existed. An app that reads how you actually feel — today, this morning, right now — and builds a plan around that. Not a plan written for someone else's perfect day. Yours.

The thing that drove every decision I made while building this is the same thing that's always driven me — I believe this should be accessible to every mum, not just the ones who can afford a PT.

A human personal trainer costs £40–80 per session. MamaFlow at £7.99 a month is 95% cheaper — and available at 3am when the baby won't sleep, on the hard days, on the good days, on every day in between.

What we believe

The values behind
every decision.

Accessibility is non-negotiable

Postpartum support should not be a privilege. MamaFlow will always have a genuinely useful free tier. A hardship access programme is built into the product from day one — quiet, private, no questions asked.

Clinical rigour, not wellness vagueness

Every exercise phase, every nutritional calculation, every recovery pathway is grounded in published evidence. Vaginal and caesarean deliveries are treated as the clinically distinct events they are. Nothing is guesswork.

The food you already eat

MamaFlow is not a meal plan. It does not ask you to swap your food for something else. It helps you understand what your food does for you — so the jollof rice, the burger, the stew — all of it works for your goals.

Your day, not someone else's

Three hours sleep is a different body than eight hours sleep. The app knows that. Every morning starts with how you actually are — and every plan is built around that. Not the ideal version of you. You, today.

What comes next

Built on clinical evidence.

Every decision in MamaFlow traces back to published evidence — RCOG guidelines, ACOG recommendations, peer-reviewed research on diastasis recti, postpartum nutrition, and breastfeeding safety. Read the full framework.
