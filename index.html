The Founder

I built this because
I needed it.

I'm a corporate professional. I coached myself from 85kg down to 73kg before pregnancy — and since my c-section I've gone from 100kg to 84kg. No fads. Just figuring out that healthy can actually taste good and consistency beats intensity every single time.

And then I had my baby by c-section. And I had absolutely no idea what to do for myself anymore. Everything I found either ignored the fact I'd had major abdominal surgery, or was built for someone with two hours and a home gym. None of it was built for me.

MamaFlow is what I wish had existed.
